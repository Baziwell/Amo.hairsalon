Box Braids Hairstyles

Box braids are a popular protective hairstyle that involves dividing the hair into square-shaped sections and braiding them. This style is versatile and can be worn in various lengths and colors. Box braids are not only stylish but also help to protect the hair from damage.

Butterfly Hairstyles

Butterfly braids are a trendy and stylish hairstyle that adds a unique twist to traditional braids. They are characterized by their loose, voluminous appearance, resembling the wings of a butterfly. This hairstyle is perfect for those looking to make a bold statement while keeping their hair protected.
